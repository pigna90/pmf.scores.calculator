PMF Scores Calculator is a web based tool that allows the simultaneous calculation of DIPSS, DIPSS-plus, MIPSS70, MIPSS70-plus version 2.0 and MTSS risk category of patients affected by Primary Myelofibrosis.
The calculator was conceived by Francesco Passamonti (University of Insubria, Varese, Italy; Hematology, ASST Sette Laghi, Varese, Italy) and Barbara Mora (Hematology, ASST Sette Laghi, Varese, Italy), complementing their scientific review entitled "Primary Myelofibrosis" (Blood. 2022).

The tool was carried out by Alessandro Romano.
